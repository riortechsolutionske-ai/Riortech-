Our Story

Started in a Nairobi bedroom. Now growing businesses nationwide.

Riortech Solutions began in 2019 when our founder, tired of watching talented Kenyan business owners get overcharged for underwhelming websites, decided to build an agency that treats every client — from a Nairobi boutique to a multi-branch retailer — like a global brand. Six years later, we've shipped over 120 websites, systems and campaigns, and we're just getting started.

Today Riortech is a full-stack digital partner: design, development, marketing, and the business software that keeps operations running. One team, one WhatsApp thread, everything handled.

Our Mission

To equip Kenyan businesses — big or small — with the digital tools, systems and strategy they need to compete and grow, without the enterprise price tag.

Our Vision

To become East Africa's most trusted digital growth partner — the first call any business makes when it's ready to go online.
